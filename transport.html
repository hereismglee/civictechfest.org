Venue

Humanities and Social Science Building (HSSB), Academia Sinica

Address

No. 128, Sec. 2, Academia Rd., Nankang, Taipei 115, Taiwan

By Bus

Take Bus 212, 276, 306, 620, 645, 679, 205, Mini Bus S5, S1, or S12 to Academica Sinica and walk to HSSB.

By MRT

Take MRT to Nankang Station, and transfer to Bus 212, 270, or B25 to Academica Sinica.

By Train

Take train to Nankang Station, and transfer to Bus 212, 270, or B25 to Academica Sinica.

By Taxi

Take taxi to Academica Sinica directly.

By Car

Get off at the exit of “Nanshen Rd.- Academia Sinica” ramp <16km> on Highway No. 3, turn left to Nanshen Rd., and turn left again to JiuZuang Rd., go straight till the intersection of Academia Rd.
